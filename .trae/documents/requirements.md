# Product Requirements Document - Delicia Kebab 2 Landing Page

## 1. Introduction
Delicia Kebab 2 is a Halal restaurant specializing in Pizzas and Kebabs, located in El Puerto de Sta María, Cádiz. The goal is to create a single-page landing page (SPA) to showcase their menu, offers, and contact information.

## 2. Business Information
- **Name:** Delicia Kebab 2
- **Location:** Pl. el Molino, Av. Sanlúcar, 5, local 9, 11500 El Puerto de Sta María, Cádiz.
- **Style:** Halal Restaurant, Fast Food (Pizza & Kebab).

## 3. Features & Sections
- **Hero Section:** High-impact image, restaurant name, and "View Menu" CTA.
- **Featured Offers:** Grid display of special deals:
    - 2 Medium Pizzas: 14.99€
    - 4 Large Pizzas + Drink: 37.99€
    - 4 Medium Pizzas + Drink: 29.99€
- **Pizza Menu:** Stylized list of 10 pizzas with ingredients. Base price: 8.50€.
    - Varieties: Margarita, Prosciutto, Tonno, Caprichosa, Funghi Prosciutto, Hawaiiana, Primavera, Shawarma, Barbacoa.
- **Location & Contact:** Address display and "Call Now" button.
- **Halal Badge:** Elegant display in header or footer.

## 4. Design Requirements
- **Palette:** Dark background (black/charcoal), vibrant yellow and red accents.
- **Typography:** Clear, readable, fast-food style.
- **Layout:** Responsive, Mobile-first.
- **Animations:** Smooth scroll animations using Framer Motion.

## 5. Technical Stack
- **Frontend:** React + TypeScript
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **Icons:** Lucide React
- **Animations:** Framer Motion
